Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Who We Are

CedCast is a multi-tenant SMS platform that helps organizations send text messages to their customers and contacts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

2. What Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Organization details (company name, address, contact information)
  • Payment information for billing purposes
  • Account login credentials
Usage Information
  • Phone numbers of your SMS recipients
  • SMS message content and delivery status
  • Website usage data and login history
  • Device and browser information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide SMS sending services to your organization
  • Process payments and manage your account balance
  • Send you important service updates and notifications
  • Improve our platform and customer support
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. SMS Data & Recipients

When you send SMS messages through CedCast:

  • We store recipient phone numbers securely for delivery purposes
  • Message content is processed only for sending and delivery tracking
  • We do not read, analyze, or use your message content for any other purpose
  • Delivery reports help you track message success rates

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data:

  • With SMS providers (Hubtel, ClickSend) to deliver your messages
  • With payment processors (Paystack) to handle billing
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Encryption for sensitive information
  • Secure servers and regular backups
  • Access controls and employee training
  • Regular security monitoring

7. Data Retention

We keep your information:

  • Account data: As long as your account is active
  • SMS logs: For 2 years for delivery tracking and support
  • Payment records: For 7 years for tax and legal purposes
  • Deleted data: Removed within 30 days of account closure

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and download your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Stop receiving marketing communications
  • Request data export or transfer

9. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage to improve our service

You can control cookie settings in your browser.

10. Third-Party Services

CedCast works with trusted partners:

  • Hubtel: Primary SMS delivery provider
  • ClickSend: Backup SMS delivery provider
  • Paystack: Secure payment processing

Each partner has their own privacy policy that applies to their services.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in Ghana and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure all transfers have appropriate security measures.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification, and update the "Last updated" date above.
